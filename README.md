# Data Analytics Dashboards

This repository contains BI analytics projects demonstrating the use of BI tools mainly, **Tableau** and **Power BI** for interactive dashboard development and business insights. These projects showcase data visualization, analytical modeling, and actionable insights generation.

---

## Projects

### 1. Tableau HR Analytics Dashboard
**Description:**  
Developed an interactive HR Analytics dashboard in **Tableau** to visualize key HR metrics for better decision-making.  

**Features & Visualizations:**  
- **Employee Attrition:** Pie charts to visualize attrition rates across departments.  
- **Demographics:** Bar charts and heatmaps showing age distribution, gender, and job roles.  
- **Job Satisfaction:** Heatmap to track satisfaction levels.  
- **Interactive Filters:** Education based filters for dynamic analysis.  

**Objective:**  
Enable HR teams to make data-driven decisions regarding employee retention, satisfaction, and workforce planning.

**Technologies Used:**  
Tableau, Excel/CSV (data source), Interactive Visualizations

---

### 2. Mall Data Analytics with Power BI
**Description:**  
Built interactive dashboards using **Power BI** to improve insights into sales, inventory, and regional performance.  

**Features & Visualizations:**  
- **Sales Insights:** Donut, line, and bar chart for potraying sales by POS, per month, and product group respectively.  
- **Inventory Management:** Heatmaps and Bar charts to monitor stock levels and turnover.
- **Payment Analysis:** Decomposition tree and ribbon chart to see payment distribution and methods.
- **Star Schema Modeling:** Applied **DAX** and **star-schema** design for efficient reporting and analytics.  
- **Interactive Filters:** Drill-down by region, product category, and time periods.  

**Objective:**  
Enable management to make informed business decisions by tracking sales, inventory, and performance metrics efficiently.

**Technologies Used:**  
Power BI, DAX, Star Schema Modeling, Excel/CSV (data source)
